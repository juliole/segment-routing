Tricorder

A tricorder in the fictional Star Trek universe is a multifunction hand-held device used for sensor scanning, data analysis, and recording data. We use the analogy for the software to deploy NFV cloud-native software to act as a Tricorder in being able to tell the health and status of the deployment as well as to be able to repair and optimize the deployment.
